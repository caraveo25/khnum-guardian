# Real-Time Shipment Tracking System

## Overview
This document outlines a real-time shipment tracking system that integrates various platforms and provides tracking capabilities based on transport modes. 

## Tracking Platforms
1. **Shippo** - A shipping software that provides access to multiple carriers and shipping options.
2. **17Track** - A global parcel tracking platform that supports multiple shipping providers.
3. **Parcelhero** - Offers delivery services and tracking information.
4. **Maersk** - A global leader in shipping and logistics.

## Tracking by Transport Mode
- **Maritime**: Follow shipments transported by sea, with updates on port departures and arrivals.
- **Air**: Track air shipments with real-time updates on flight information and delivery schedules.
- **Customs**: Monitor customs clearance processes and requirements for each shipment.

## Red Alert Risks
1. **Delay in Customs Clearance**
2. **Damage to Goods During Transit**
3. **Misdelivery or Lost Shipments**
4. **Carrier Service Interruptions**
5. **Weather-Related Delays**
6. **Regulatory Changes Impacting Transport**

## Standard Timeline
- The standard timeline for shipment delivery is typically between **25 to 35 days**, depending on various factors including transport mode and distance.
